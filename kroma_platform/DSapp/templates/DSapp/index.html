This section allows you to explore and filter articles retrieved from PubMed Central that mention Dravet Syndrome or Severe Myoclonic Epilepsy of Infancy.

  • Not all articles are Dravet-focused. Some papers include Dravet Syndrome as part of a broader epilepsy cohort.
  • To view articles that specifically study Dravet Syndrome, select “Yes” in the DS-related filter on the left.
  • Article classification and annotation are still in progress. Organism and clinical-axis filters/charts are temporarily disabled until curation is complete.

Recommended workflow:

  1. Use the filters on the left to narrow the articles of interest.
  2. Click Apply filters to update the table.
  3. Select one or more articles from the table.
  4. Click Show details for selected articles.
