Welcome to KroMA

KroMA is an AI assistant that reasons over a curated knowledge graph built from full-text biomedical literature and expert ontologies. It is currently optimized for Dravet syndrome but is designed to extend to broader neurological disorders.

For clinicians & researchers
  • Evidence-grounded answers for diagnosis and management
  • Summaries of original DS studies by axis (seizures, genetics, pharmacology, etc.)
For patients & families
  • Plain-language explanations of complex concepts
  • Contextualized responses informed by the DS knowledge graph
For basic scientists
  • Structured triples extracted from DS literature and EpSO
  • Support for hypothesis generation and gap-finding
